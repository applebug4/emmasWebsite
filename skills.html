one skill i want to share is kandi!

☮ what is kandi? - kandi is a type of beaded craft that primarily uses pony beads and other plastic beads. it is most commonly associated with jewelry but you can make bags, charms, and even clothes with it!

☮ i like making kandi because it goes very well with my outfits and i find it to be a very creative and relaxing process!

☮ i consider this a skill of mine because it took time to get to my level of skill! i started off making single bracelets and smaller cuffs and now i have made many different things!

☮ some examples of things i have made include:

  • rotating cuffs! as shown in the video! these are bracelets that have a rotating component to them, which are very fun to fidget with!
  • multistitch cuffs! these are good for patterns, such as leopard print, or a frog! they're like pixel art, with each bead being a pixel!
  • flower cuffs! these cuffs resemble a chain of flowers! they're really cute!
  • ladder cuffs! these are what they sound like, they are made vertically like a ladder, rather than horizontally like most bracelets.
  • x-base cuffs! these are quite versatile and are used as the based for many kinds of cuffs!
  • these are not shown, but i've made some other cool things are aren't jewelry including: headbands, goggles, a tie, and keychains

☮ some things i still want to make include:

  • a purse! i've yet to find a rainbow purse so it would be really cool to make my own so that i can make it the perfect size!
  • a slinky cuff. i've been putting off making this! i have the materials but for some reason i'm a bit hesitant!
  • a skirt! this is a big project but kandi skirts look so cute!
